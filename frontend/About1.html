Welcome to Period Tracker

Your personal companion to track menstrual cycles, predict periods, log food, and receive reminders.

📖 About This App

Period Tracker helps women manage menstrual health by predicting cycles, tracking data, and considering age-based changes. Stay informed and healthy with personalized insights. This project is a Period Tracker Web App built for women’s health management. It allows users to record their period dates and predict the next cycle. The app also considers cycle length variations and ensures realistic ranges. Age factor is included to adjust predictions for teenagers, adults, and older women. A homepage explains what the app does and how to use it. Users can sign up, log in, and store their personal data securely in the browser. Each user only sees their own history, not others’. A history page shows period predictions and food logs together.

✨ How to Use

  • 📝 Sign Up / Login – Create an account or log in to access your data.
  • 👩 Set Your Age – Age helps adjust predictions for accuracy.
  • 📅 Enter Last Period Date – Input the start date of your last cycle.
  • 🔢 Add Cycle Length – Usually 28 days, but customize to your cycle.
  • 📊 View Predictions – The app calculates your next expected period.
  • 🩸 Track History – Check past cycles in the History section.
  • 🥗 Log Food – Record cravings or meals to observe health patterns.
  • 🔔 Enable Notifications – Click "Get Notified" to receive daily reminders.
  • 👤 Check Profile – Update personal details anytime in the Profile page.
  • 🌸 Stay Consistent – Regular logging improves cycle prediction accuracy.
